## Overview

Account Settings let you manage your SmartAlex profile, security options, and account preferences.

## Profile Settings

### Personal Information

Update your account details:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Full Name** | Your display name |
| **Email** | Login email (requires verification to change) |
| **Phone** | Contact phone number |
| **Timezone** | Your local timezone for scheduling |

### Organization

If you're part of a team:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Organization Name** | Your company or team name |
| **Industry** | Your business type (for tailored features) |
| **Website** | Company website URL |

## Security

### Password

Change your password:
1. Enter current password
2. Enter new password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Confirm new password
4. Click **Update Password**

<Note>
  Password requirements:
  - Minimum 8 characters
  - At least one uppercase letter
  - At least one number
  - At least one special character
</Note>

### Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Add an extra layer of security:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable MFA">
    Go to **Settings** → **Security** → **Enable MFA**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Scan QR Code">
    Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password) to scan the QR code.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Verification Code">
    Enter the 6-digit code from your authenticator app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save Backup Codes">
    Download and securely store your backup codes for account recovery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Keep your backup codes safe. If you lose access to your authenticator app and backup codes, you may be locked out of your account.
</Warning>

### Active Sessions

View and manage devices logged into your account:
- Device type and browser
- Location (approximate)
- Last active time
- Revoke access if needed

## Notifications

Configure how SmartAlex communicates with you:

### Email Notifications

| Notification | Description |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Campaign Complete** | When a campaign finishes |
| **Usage Alerts** | When approaching plan limits |
| **Billing** | Payment receipts and reminders |
| **Product Updates** | New features and announcements |

### In-App Notifications

Control which notifications appear in the dashboard:
- System alerts
- Campaign status updates
- Callback reminders

## Privacy

### Data Management

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Export Data** | Download all your account data (GDPR) |
| **Delete Recordings** | Remove call recordings |
| **Delete Account** | Permanently delete your account |

<Warning>
  Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data including agents, campaigns, contacts, and call history will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

### GDPR Compliance

SmartAlex supports GDPR data rights:
- **Right to Access**: Export your data anytime
- **Right to Rectification**: Update your information
- **Right to Erasure**: Delete your account and data
- **Data Portability**: Export in standard formats

## Team Management

### Team Members

Invite team members to your organization:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Invite">
    Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Role">
    Choose their permission level:
    - **Admin**: Full access including billing
    - **Manager**: Manage agents and campaigns
    - **Viewer**: Read-only access to analytics
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    The invitee receives an email to join your organization.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Team Limits

Team member limits vary by plan:

| Plan | Team Members |
|------|-------------|
| Professional | 3 |
| Dental | 10 |
| Real Estate | Unlimited |

## API Access

### API Keys

Generate API keys for integration:

1. Go to **Settings** → **API**
2. Click **Generate New Key**
3. Copy and securely store the key
4. The key is only shown once

<Warning>
  Treat API keys like passwords. Never share them publicly or commit them to version control.
</Warning>

### Webhooks

Configure webhook endpoints to receive real-time events:
- Call completed
- Campaign status changed
- Contact updated
- Callback scheduled

See [API Webhooks](/api-reference/webhooks) for configuration details.
