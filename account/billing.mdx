## Overview

Manage your SmartAlex subscription, view invoices, and add prepaid credits through the Billing page.

## Subscription

### Current Plan

View your active subscription:
- Plan name and tier
- Monthly price
- Included features
- Renewal date

### Changing Plans

Upgrade or downgrade your subscription:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Billing">
    Navigate to **Account** → **Billing**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Change Plan">
    View available plans and compare features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select New Plan">
    Choose your desired plan.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Change">
    - **Upgrades**: Take effect immediately, prorated for current period
    - **Downgrades**: Take effect at next billing date
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Cancellation

To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to **Billing** → **Cancel Subscription**
2. Complete the cancellation survey
3. Confirm cancellation

<Note>
  When you cancel, your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. You won't be charged again, and you can continue using SmartAlex until then.
</Note>

## Payment Methods

### Adding a Card

1. Go to **Billing** → **Payment Methods**
2. Click **Add Payment Method**
3. Enter card details
4. Click **Save**

Accepted cards:
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Discover

### Default Payment Method

Your default payment method is used for:
- Monthly subscription charges
- Automatic wallet top-ups
- Overage charges

To change default:
1. Go to **Payment Methods**
2. Click the menu on the card you want as default
3. Select **Make Default**

### Removing a Card

You cannot remove your only payment method while you have an active subscription. Add a new card first, then remove the old one.

## Invoices

### Viewing Invoices

Access all invoices from **Billing** → **Invoices**:
- Invoice date
- Amount charged
- Payment status
- Download PDF

### Invoice Details

Each invoice includes:
- Subscription charges
- Usage charges (overage)
- Wallet top-ups
- Phone number fees
- Taxes (if applicable)

### Receipts

Payment receipts are automatically emailed to your account email. Download past receipts from the invoice detail page.

## Wallet

The Wallet is a prepaid credit system for usage beyond your plan's included minutes.

### How It Works

1. **Add credits** to your wallet
2. **Usage charges** are deducted automatically
3. **Low balance alerts** notify you when to top up

### Adding Credits

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Wallet">
    Navigate to **Account** → **Wallet**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Amount">
    Choose a preset amount or enter custom:
    - $25
    - $50
    - $100
    - Custom amount (minimum $10)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Payment">
    Use your default payment method or add a new card.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Credits are added immediately and never expire.

### Auto Top-Up

Enable automatic top-ups to never run out of credits:

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Threshold** | When balance falls below this amount |
| **Top-up Amount** | Amount to automatically add |
| **Payment Method** | Card to charge for top-ups |

### Wallet Balance

View your current balance and recent transactions:
- Current balance
- Recent charges (call costs)
- Recent top-ups
- Transaction history

## Usage & Costs

### Understanding Usage

Usage is measured in calling minutes:

| Call Type | Cost per Minute |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Inbound calls | $0.11 |
| Outbound calls | $0.11 |
| Web calls | $0.08 |

### Included Minutes

Each plan includes monthly minutes:

| Plan | Included Minutes |
|------|-----------------|
| Professional | 100 |
| Dental | 300 |
| Real Estate | 300 |

### Overage

When you exceed included minutes:
1. Charges are deducted from your wallet
2. If wallet is empty, charges are billed to your card
3. No service interruption

### Usage Tracking

Monitor usage in real-time:
- Minutes used this period
- Minutes remaining
- Projected monthly usage
- Cost breakdown by call type

## Billing FAQ

<Accordion title="When am I charged?">
Your subscription renews monthly on the anniversary of your signup date. Usage overage and wallet charges may occur anytime during the month.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable. Wallet credits can be refunded within 30 days if unused. Contact support for refund requests.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I update my billing email?">
Invoices are sent to your account email. Update your email in Account Settings to change where invoices are sent.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
Yes. SmartAlex uses Stripe for payment processing. We never store your full card number - only a secure token.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer annual billing?">
Contact sales for annual billing options with discounted rates.
</Accordion>
