## About Switch Telecom

Johannesburg-based hosted PBX provider offering the Atria platform. Common with South African enterprise and mid-market, particularly in financial services and professional services verticals.

## Step 1: create trunk in SmartAlex

SIP Trunk wizard, pick **Other**. Atria speaks standard SIP. Save credentials.

## Step 2: Switch Telecom portal configuration

Same two options as with other hosted PBX providers.

### Option A: forward a DID

1. Log in to the Atria portal
2. Navigate to the DID management section
3. Select the target number
4. Set destination to external forward, pointing at a SmartAlex-provisioned DID

Simplest path. AI answers via our DID.

### Option B: SIP trunk from Atria

Switch Telecom can provision an outbound SIP trunk from Atria to an external endpoint. Contact Switch Telecom support and request:

- **External SIP trunk to**: `sip.voice.getsmartalex.com`
- **Authentication**: credentials from SmartAlex
- **Codecs**: G.711 u-law, A-law
- **Transport**: UDP port 5060
- **REFER support**: enabled

Lead time depends on your account manager; usually 1 to 5 business days.

## Step 3: extensions and agent

Populate SmartAlex extensions (for Option B). Link agent.

## Step 4: test

Dial, transfer.

## Switch Telecom-specific notes

- **Atria vs their legacy platform**: this guide covers Atria. Older Switch Telecom customers on the legacy platform should contact Switch Telecom to migrate to Atria first, then follow this guide.
- **Caller ID**: Atria handles P-Asserted-Identity correctly on forwarded calls when configured. Confirm with Switch Telecom support.
- **Call recording**: Atria has comprehensive recording features. Coordinate with Switch Telecom on whether Atria or SmartAlex is the primary recording source.
- **Enterprise peering**: Switch Telecom offers direct peering options for high-volume customers. Discuss with your account manager.
