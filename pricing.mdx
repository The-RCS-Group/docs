## Usage-based pricing

Once your plan's included minutes are used, additional usage is deducted from your prepaid wallet at these rates:

| Item | Rate |
|---|---|
| Web calls (widget) | **$0.08/min** |
| Phone calls (inbound or outbound) | **$0.11/min** |
| Additional phone numbers | **$1/month** each |
| Additional concurrency slot | **$9.60/slot/month** |

How wallet billing works: top up your wallet with credits, plan minutes are consumed first, then wallet credits cover any overage automatically.

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the free trial work?">
    Start with a 7-day free trial on any plan. Your included minutes are available immediately so you can place real calls.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when I exceed my included minutes?">
    Additional minutes are charged at $0.08/min for web calls and $0.11/min for phone calls, deducted automatically from your prepaid wallet balance.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is concurrency?">
    Concurrency is how many calls your AI can handle at the same time. Professional and Real Estate include 10 slots. Add more at $9.60/slot/month if you need them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I switch plans or cancel anytime?">
    Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel from your account settings at any time. No long-term contracts or cancellation fees on Professional. Enterprise contracts are negotiated per customer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How does wallet-based billing work?">
    Add credits to your wallet and usage is deducted automatically. Your plan's included minutes are used first, then wallet credits cover any overages.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer annual discounts?">
    Yes. Pay annually on Professional or Real Estate to save vs. monthly billing. Enterprise offers custom annual terms.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
    All major credit cards via Stripe on Professional, Dental, and Real Estate. Enterprise supports invoice billing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there setup fees?">
    None on the standard plans. Enterprise deployments with significant custom integration work may include a project fee, agreed upfront.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
