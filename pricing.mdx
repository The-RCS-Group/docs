## Usage Pricing

Beyond your included minutes, usage is charged per minute:

| Call Type | Cost |
|-----------|------|

| **Inbound Phone Calls** | $0.11/min |
| **Outbound Phone Calls** | $0.11/min |

Usage charges are deducted from your wallet balance or charged to your payment method.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="What happens if I exceed my included minutes?">
Additional minutes are charged at the usage rates above. Charges are deducted from your wallet balance first. If your wallet is empty, they're charged to your payment method.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at your next billing date.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a contract or commitment?">
No. All plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do unused minutes roll over?">
No. Included minutes reset each billing cycle and do not roll over.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) via Stripe.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do you offer discounts for annual billing?">
Yes. Contact our sales team for annual billing options with discounted rates.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a setup fee?">
No. There are no setup fees or hidden costs.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I try before I buy?">
Yes. Start with our free trial - no credit card required. You'll have 7 days and 10 minutes to test SmartAlex.
</Accordion>
