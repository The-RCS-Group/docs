## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the $99 per agent work?">
    The Standard plan includes one agent for $99/month. Each additional agent you deploy is another $99/month. Agents can share knowledge bases, phone numbers, and tools across the tenant.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What counts as an agent?">
    One agent is one configured AI persona: one voice, one system prompt, one set of tools. If you run a medical practice with one AI answering all calls, that's one agent. If you want a separate AI for outbound campaigns with a different voice and script, that's a second agent.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I change plans anytime?">
    Yes. Standard is month to month. Move to Enterprise anytime by contacting sales. Downgrades take effect on the next billing date.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a contract?">
    Standard is month to month. Enterprise contracts are negotiated per customer.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do you offer annual discounts?">
    Yes on Enterprise. Contact sales.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What payment methods?">
    Standard: all major credit cards via Stripe. Enterprise: invoice billing.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Setup fees?">
    None on Standard. Enterprise deployments with significant custom integration work may have a project fee, agreed upfront.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a trial?">
    Yes. 7 days, full platform access, no credit card required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
