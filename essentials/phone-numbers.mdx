## Getting Phone Numbers

SmartAlex provides phone numbers from multiple countries. When someone calls your number, they're connected to your AI agent.

### Available Countries

| Country | Coverage | Example Format |
|---------|----------|----------------|
| United States | All area codes | +1 (555) 123-4567 |
| United Kingdom | All area codes | +44 20 1234 5678 |
| Australia | Major cities | +61 2 1234 5678 |
| South Africa | Major cities | +27 11 123 4567 |
| Singapore | National | +65 1234 5678 |

### Pricing

Every plan includes **1 free phone number for your first month**. After the first month, phone numbers are billed monthly based on region:

| Region | Monthly Cost |
|--------|--------------|
| United States | $1.15/month |
| United Kingdom | $1.50/month |
| Australia | $1.85/month |
| South Africa | $2.00/month |
| Singapore | $2.50/month |

<Note>
Your first phone number is free during your first month on any plan. Additional numbers and all numbers after the first month are billed at the regional rates above.
</Note>

## Purchasing a Number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Phone Numbers">
    Go to **Phone Numbers** in your dashboard.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Get New Number">
    Click the **Get New Number** button.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Country and Area">
    Choose your country and preferred area code. Available numbers for that area will be shown.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a Number">
    Click on the number you want. The number is provisioned immediately.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign to Agent">
    Select which AI agent should answer calls to this number.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing Numbers

### Number List

Your phone numbers page shows:
- Phone number
- Assigned agent (if any)
- Monthly cost
- Date acquired

### Actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Assign Agent** | Connect the number to an AI agent |
| **Change Agent** | Switch to a different agent |
| **Unassign** | Remove agent (number won't answer) |
| **Release** | Give up the number (cannot be undone) |

<Warning>
  Releasing a number is permanent. The number will be returned to the pool and may be assigned to another customer.
</Warning>

## Number Assignment

Each phone number can be assigned to one AI agent at a time. The agent handles all inbound calls to that number.

You can:
- Assign one number to multiple campaigns using the same agent
- Have multiple numbers for the same agent (e.g., different area codes)
- Change assignments at any time without losing the number

<Note>
  When you change which agent is assigned to a number, the change takes effect immediately. No downtime or reconfiguration needed.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Choosing Area Codes">
Select area codes that make sense for your business:
- **Local presence**: Use area codes where your customers are
- **Toll-free**: Consider 800 numbers for national businesses
- **Multiple markets**: Get numbers in each major market you serve
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Number Organization">
Keep your numbers organized:
- Name agents clearly (e.g., "Main Line", "Sales", "Support")
- Document which number is for what purpose
- Review unused numbers monthly to avoid unnecessary charges
</Accordion>
