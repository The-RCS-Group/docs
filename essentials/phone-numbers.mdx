## Getting Phone Numbers

SmartAlex provisions phone numbers from your preferred area codes.

### Pricing

| Type | Price |
|------|-------|
| US Numbers | $1/month |
| International | $1.50/month |

## Managing Numbers

Navigate to **Phone Numbers** in your dashboard to:
- Browse available numbers by area code
- Assign numbers to agents
- Release unused numbers
- View monthly costs

## Number Assignment

Each phone number can be assigned to one AI agent. When someone calls that number, they'll be connected to your agent.

<Note>
  You can change which agent is assigned to a number at any time without losing the number.
</Note>
