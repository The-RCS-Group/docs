## What is a Campaign?

A Campaign lets you call hundreds or thousands of contacts using your AI agent. Perfect for:
- Lead outreach
- Appointment reminders
- Customer surveys
- Follow-up calls

## Creating a Campaign

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Campaign">
    Go to **Campaigns** and click **Create Campaign**. Give it a name and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Agent">
    Choose which AI agent will make the calls.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Contacts">
    Import a CSV with phone numbers and contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    Set call timing, language, and campaign objectives.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch">
    Start your campaign and monitor progress in real-time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign Status

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Draft** | Not yet started |
| **Active** | Currently running |
| **Paused** | Temporarily stopped |
| **Completed** | All contacts called |

## Automatic Callbacks

SmartAlex automatically schedules callbacks for:
- Voicemail detections
- No-answer calls
- Busy signals
- Network timeouts
