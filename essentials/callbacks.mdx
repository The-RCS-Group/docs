## How Callbacks Work

When a call doesn't connect, SmartAlex automatically schedules a callback:

| Trigger | Action |
|---------|--------|
| **Voicemail** | Retry in 24 hours |
| **No Answer** | Retry in 24 hours |
| **Busy Signal** | Retry in 1 hour |
| **Network Error** | Retry in 30 minutes |
| **Invalid Number** | Marked failed (no retry) |

## Managing Callbacks

Navigate to **Callbacks** to see all pending callbacks:
- **Due Today** - Calls scheduled for today
- **Overdue** - Missed callback windows
- **This Week** - Upcoming callbacks

## Manual Callbacks

Your AI agent can also schedule callbacks during a call when a customer requests to be called back at a specific time.

<Note>
  Callbacks are automatically deduplicated - you won't call the same person twice for the same campaign.
</Note>
