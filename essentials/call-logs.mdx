## Call Analytics

Every call is logged with detailed analytics:

| Data | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Recording** | Full audio playback |
| **Transcript** | AI-generated conversation text |
| **Duration** | Call length |
| **Sentiment** | Positive, neutral, or negative |
| **Outcome** | Completed, voicemail, transferred, etc. |
| **Cost** | Per-call cost breakdown |

## Viewing Calls

Navigate to **Call Logs** to see all calls. Click any call to open the detail drawer with:
- Full transcript
- Recording playback
- Contact information
- Campaign details (if applicable)

## Sentiment Analysis

SmartAlex automatically analyzes the emotional tone of each call:

- **Positive** - Customer expressed satisfaction or interest
- **Neutral** - Standard informational call
- **Negative** - Customer frustrated or dissatisfied

Use sentiment data to identify training opportunities and track customer satisfaction.
