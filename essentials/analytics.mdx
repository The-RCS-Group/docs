## Overview

Analytics gives you deep insight into how your AI agents are performing. Navigate to **Analytics** in your dashboard to dig into metrics across calls, campaigns, costs, and quality.

## Tabs

The Analytics page is organised into focused tabs:

| Tab | What you see |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Key metrics across all calls — total calls, success rate, average duration, sentiment distribution |
| **Campaigns** | Per-campaign performance, contacts reached, connect rates, costs |
| **Costs & ROI** | Spend breakdown by call type and agent, projected costs, cost per successful call |
| **Quality** | Call sentiment trends, transcript-level quality signals |
| **Operations** | Operational metrics for managing capacity and throughput |
| **Attribution** | Marketing attribution: which channels drive calls and conversions |

<Note>
  Some tabs (Campaigns, Attribution) may be hidden on inbound-only plans where they don't apply.
</Note>

## Date ranges

Use the date picker to scope all charts and tables. Presets cover today, the last 7 / 30 days, the current and previous month. Pick a custom range from the calendar for arbitrary windows.

## Overview metrics

The Overview tab surfaces these at a glance:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| **Total Calls** | All calls in the selected window (inbound + outbound + web) |
| **Success rate** | Percentage of calls that connected and met the success criteria |
| **Average duration** | Mean call length |
| **Cost** | Spend across the window plus per-call and per-success cost |
| **Sentiment distribution** | Positive / neutral / negative split, shown as a pie chart |

Charts include sentiment pies, call-volume bar charts, and duration trend areas.

## Sentiment

Each call is automatically tagged as **Positive**, **Neutral**, or **Negative** based on the conversation. Use sentiment to:
- Identify configurations that delight callers
- Spot prompts or scripts that frustrate them
- Track satisfaction over time as you iterate

## Costs & ROI

The Costs tab breaks spend down by:
- **Call category** — outbound, inbound, web (widget), and test calls
- **Per-channel** — average cost per call and per successful call

Use it to see where your minutes are going and which channels are the most cost-efficient.

## Inspecting individual calls

Click any call row in a table to open the call details panel with:
- Full transcript with speaker labels
- Recording playback
- Caller / contact info
- AI-generated summary

## Exporting data

Export filtered call records to CSV from the relevant table. Use exports for executive reporting, external analysis, or reconciling against your billing.
