## What is an AI Agent?

An AI Agent is your virtual receptionist or sales representative. Each agent has its own personality, voice, and behavior settings.

## Creating an Agent

Navigate to **Agents** in your dashboard and click **Create Agent**.

### Configuration Options

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Name** | Your agent's name (e.g., "Alex") |
| **Voice** | Choose from 50+ natural voices |
| **Language** | English, Spanish, French, and more |
| **System Prompt** | Define what your agent should do |
| **AI Model** | GPT-4, Claude, or Gemini |

### Agent Status

Agents progress through these states:
- **Draft** - Still being configured
- **Creating** - Being provisioned
- **Ready** - Live and ready to take calls

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Writing Effective System Prompts">
Be specific about what your agent should do:
- Collect customer information
- Schedule appointments
- Answer FAQs about your business
- Qualify leads before transferring
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Choosing the Right Voice">
Match your voice to your brand:
- Professional services → Calm, authoritative voices
- Retail/hospitality → Friendly, upbeat voices
- Healthcare → Warm, reassuring voices
</Accordion>
