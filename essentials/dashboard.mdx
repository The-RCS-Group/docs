## Overview

Your dashboard is a chat-first home that puts Alex (your in-app assistant) at the centre. From here you can ask Alex to do things, work through your setup checklist, and jump straight to recent calls.

## Greeting and chat

The dashboard opens with a personalised greeting and a chat composer. Type what you want to do — for example, *"create a new agent for after-hours support"* or *"show me last week's calls"* — and Alex helps you get there.

## Setup checklist

A dropdown checklist tracks the essentials for getting your account live. It auto-completes as you finish each step:

| Step | Done when |
|---|---|
| **Create your AI agent** | At least one agent exists on your account |
| **Test your agent** | At least one call (any source) has been made |
| **Connect a phone number** | A number is assigned to one of your agents |
| **Import your contacts** | At least one contact has been uploaded *(hidden on inbound-only plans)* |

The number of completed steps shows in the dropdown header so you always know what's left.

## Trial banner

If you're on the 7-day free trial, a banner shows your trial status and end date. Click through to upgrade at any point during the trial.

## Recent activity

The activity section lists your most recent calls so you can quickly review what's been happening. Each row shows:
- Call source (inbound, outbound, or web)
- Outcome
- Duration
- When it happened

Click any row to open the full call record with transcript and recording.

## Suggested campaigns

If your plan includes outbound campaigns and you have contacts uploaded, the dashboard surfaces suggested campaigns you can launch in one click — pulled from your existing contact lists and agent configuration.
