## Create Your First AI Agent

Follow these steps to set up your SmartAlex AI voice agent.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your SmartAlex account at [getsmartalex.com/instant](https://getsmartalex.com/instant).

    New accounts start with a **7-day free trial** with full access to all features.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Setup Checklist">
    After signing in, you'll see the setup checklist on your dashboard. This guides you through:
    - Creating your first agent
    - Getting a phone number
    - Testing your agent
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Your AI Agent">
    Navigate to **Agents** and click **Create Agent**. The wizard guides you through three steps:

    1. **Describe** - Tell the AI what your agent should do
    2. **Customize** - Choose voice, language, and personality
    3. **Deploy** - Review and create your agent
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get a Phone Number">
    Go to **Phone Numbers** and click **Buy New**:
    - Select your country (US, Canada, UK, Australia, New Zealand, Netherlands, Denmark, Finland, or South Africa)
    - Pick from available local, mobile, or toll-free numbers
    - Numbers are **$1/month** plus per-minute usage
  </Step>

  <Step title="Test Your Agent">
    Use the **Quick Test** button on any agent to have a conversation before going live. This uses web calling so you can test without a phone.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Call Types

SmartAlex handles two types of calls:

| Type | Description | Cost |
|------|-------------|------|
| **Inbound** | Someone calls your SmartAlex number | $0.11/min |
| **Web Calls** | Click-to-call from your website widget | $0.08/min |
| **Outbound** | Your agent calls a contact (campaigns) | $0.11/min |
