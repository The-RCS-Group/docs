## Create Your First AI Agent

Follow these steps to set up your first SmartAlex AI voice agent.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your SmartAlex account at [app.smartalex.ai](https://app.smartalex.ai).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an Agent">
    Navigate to the Agents page and click "Create Agent". Give your agent a name and personality.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a Phone Number">
    Go to Phone Numbers and select an area code. SmartAlex will provision a number for your agent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Agent">
    Use the "Quick Test" button to have a conversation with your agent before going live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
