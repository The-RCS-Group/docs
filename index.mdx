## What is SmartAlex?

SmartAlex is an AI voice assistant platform that helps businesses automate phone communications 24/7. Create custom AI agents that can handle inbound calls, schedule appointments, qualify leads, capture customer information, and run outbound calling campaigns.

## Why SmartAlex?

SmartAlex gives businesses an AI-powered phone system that works around the clock with no missed calls, no hold times, and no staffing headaches.

- **50+ natural voices** across multiple voice providers including Cartesia, ElevenLabs, OpenAI, and Deepgram
- **GPT-4o, Claude, and Gemini AI models** powering intelligent, context-aware conversations
- **Phone numbers in 5+ countries** including the US, UK, Australia, South Africa, and Singapore
- **99.9% uptime** so your business never misses a call
- **7-day free trial, no credit card required** to evaluate the platform risk-free
- **REST API + MCP server** for seamless AI agent integration and automation workflows
- **Campaign management** with automatic retry logic, scheduling, and real-time monitoring
- **Real-time call analytics** with full transcripts, call recordings, sentiment analysis, and AI-generated summaries

## Get Started in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your AI agent">
    Describe what your agent should do in plain language. Choose a voice, select an AI model, and upload any knowledge base documents. SmartAlex configures everything automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a phone number">
    Pick a dedicated phone number in the US, UK, Australia, South Africa, or Singapore. Assign it to your agent with one click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Your agent starts answering calls immediately. Monitor performance in real time from your dashboard, review transcripts, and refine as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

- **Dental practices** - Appointment scheduling and patient inquiries
- **Real estate agencies** - Lead qualification and property information
- **Professional services** - Client intake and scheduling
- **Small businesses** - After-hours call handling
