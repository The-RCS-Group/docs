## What is SmartAlex?

SmartAlex is an AI voice assistant platform that helps businesses automate phone communications 24/7. Create custom AI agents that handle inbound calls, schedule appointments, qualify leads, capture customer information, and run outbound calling campaigns.

## Why SmartAlex?

- **Natural, human-sounding voices** across a wide range of accents and character options
- **Advanced conversational AI** for context-aware, intelligent interactions
- **Buy phone numbers in 9 countries**: United States, Canada, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Netherlands, Denmark, Finland, South Africa
- **Integrates with your existing PBX**: 3CX, Yeastar, FreePBX, Asterisk, Avaya, Mitel, Microsoft Teams, and more
- **99.9% uptime SLA** on Enterprise plans so your business never misses a call
- **7-day free trial** on all plans, no credit card required to explore the product
- **REST API and MCP server** for AI assistant and automation integration
- **Campaign management** with retry logic, scheduling, and real-time monitoring
- **Real-time call analytics** with transcripts, recordings, sentiment analysis, and AI-generated summaries

## Get Started in 3 Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your AI agent">
    Describe what your agent should do in plain language. Choose a voice, upload any knowledge base documents. SmartAlex configures everything automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a phone number">
    Pick a dedicated phone number in any of 9 supported countries. Assign it to your agent with one click. Or connect your existing PBX via SIP trunk.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    Your agent starts answering calls immediately. Monitor performance in real time from your dashboard, review transcripts, and refine as needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Who is SmartAlex For?

- **Dental and medical practices**: appointment scheduling, patient enquiries
- **Real estate agencies**: lead qualification, property information, viewing scheduling
- **Professional services**: client intake, conflict checks, scheduling
- **Dealerships**: service status enquiries, parts enquiries, after-hours coverage
- **Small businesses**: after-hours call handling, receptionist replacement
- **Enterprise call centres**: IVR replacement, queue qualification, multi-site deployment
