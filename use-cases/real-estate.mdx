## Key Use Cases

### 1. Inbound Lead Qualification

When a prospect calls about a listing, the AI agent handles the full qualification conversation:

- Greets the caller and identifies which property they are inquiring about
- Asks qualifying questions: budget range, pre-approval status, timeline to purchase, areas of interest
- Captures contact details and preferred contact method
- Routes qualified leads to the appropriate agent based on territory or specialization
- Sends the agent a summary with all qualification data before they call back

<Note>
  Agencies using SmartAlex report that AI-qualified leads convert at 2-3x the rate of unqualified inbound calls because agents spend their time on serious buyers.
</Note>

### 2. Property Information Calls

Buyers calling about specific listings get immediate, detailed responses:

- Property specifications (bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, lot size)
- Pricing and recent price changes
- Neighborhood information and nearby amenities
- HOA fees, tax information, and estimated monthly payments
- Availability for viewings

The AI pulls property data from your CRM or listing database and delivers it conversationally, just like a knowledgeable agent would.

## Sample Agent Prompt

Here is an example system prompt for a real estate agency AI voice agent:

```text
You are Sarah, the AI assistant for Prestige Realty Group. You handle inbound
calls from buyers, sellers, and tenants with a warm, professional tone.

Your primary responsibilities:
1. Qualify buyer leads by asking about budget, timeline, pre-approval status,
   preferred neighborhoods, and property type (house, condo, townhouse).
2. Provide property information from our active listings when callers ask
   about specific addresses or MLS numbers.
3. Book property viewings by checking agent availability and confirming
   appointments.
4. For seller inquiries, capture the property address, reason for selling,
   and desired timeline, then schedule a listing consultation.
5. For tenant maintenance requests, log the issue, assess urgency, and
   route emergencies to the on-call maintenance team.

Qualifying questions for buyers:
- "What areas are you most interested in?"
- "Do you have a budget range in mind?"
- "Have you been pre-approved for a mortgage?"
- "What is your ideal timeline for moving?"
- "Are you currently working with another agent?"

Always capture: full name, phone number, email address.

Transfer rules:
- Hot leads (pre-approved, ready to buy within 30 days) → transfer immediately
  to the duty agent
- General inquiries → schedule a callback within 4 business hours
- Maintenance emergencies (flooding, no heat, security) → transfer to
  on-call team immediately
```
