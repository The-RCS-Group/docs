# SmartAlex vs Synthflow: Which AI Voice Agent Platform Fits Your Business?

SmartAlex and Synthflow both offer no-code AI voice agent platforms, but they target different use cases. **SmartAlex is a deep business platform** that integrates voice agents with CRM, campaign management, analytics, billing, and vertical-specific tools like LaunchPad for real estate. **Synthflow is a no-code builder focused on simplicity and white-label capabilities**, making it popular with agencies that resell voice AI to their clients.

**Choose SmartAlex** if you need a comprehensive platform with integrated CRM, outbound campaign management, advanced analytics, and real estate tools, or if you want API and MCP access for deeper integrations.

**Choose Synthflow** if you are an agency looking for white-label voice AI to resell, want the simplest possible no-code setup, or need a straightforward builder without the complexity of a full platform.

## Platform Overview

### What Is SmartAlex?

SmartAlex is an end-to-end AI voice agent platform built for businesses that need more than just voice agents. The platform combines a visual agent builder (with a Standard URL-based path and an Advanced wizard, plus Squad Architect for multi-agent designs) with a full-featured CRM, outbound campaign management, real-time analytics dashboards, Stripe billing with wallet and trial management, embeddable website widgets, and a mobile app for iOS and Android.

What sets SmartAlex apart from other voice agent platforms is the depth of its business tooling. The built-in CRM tracks every contact interaction across calls, campaigns, and messaging channels. The campaign management system handles outbound calling operations with scheduling, retry logic, disposition tracking, and performance analytics. And LaunchPad provides purpose-built tools for real estate, including a reservation management system with 15+ status states, buyer portals, developer dashboards, and commission tracking.

SmartAlex also offers an MCP (Model Context Protocol) server with 28 tools, which allows AI assistants like Claude and ChatGPT to manage the platform through natural language, a capability no other voice agent platform provides.

### What Is Synthflow?

Synthflow is a no-code AI voice agent builder designed for simplicity and accessibility. The platform uses a drag-and-drop flow builder that makes it possible to create voice agents without any technical background. Synthflow has gained attention from TechCrunch and other media outlets for making voice AI accessible to non-technical users.

Synthflow's white-label capabilities make it especially popular with agencies and resellers. The platform allows agencies to remove Synthflow branding, apply their own branding, host the platform on custom domains, and manage sub-accounts for individual clients. This white-label model is core to Synthflow's positioning, making it the go-to platform for agencies that want to resell voice AI under their own brand.

Synthflow integrates with common business tools through Zapier and Make, providing access to thousands of apps without custom development. The platform includes templates for common use cases like customer service, appointment booking, and lead qualification.

## White-Label & Agency Features

This is a critical comparison point since both platforms serve agencies, but with fundamentally different approaches.

### SmartAlex Workspace Model

SmartAlex uses workspace-level isolation designed for agencies and multi-location businesses:

- **Workspace isolation**: Each client operates in a fully isolated workspace with their own data, agents, contacts, and campaigns. Data never crosses workspace boundaries.
- **Role-based access control**: Owner, Admin, Member, and Viewer roles with configurable permissions per workspace
- **Embeddable widgets**: Customizable voice agent widgets that can be embedded on client websites with per-workspace branding
- **Client-specific billing**: Integrated Stripe billing allows per-client subscription management, wallet top-ups, and trial periods
- **Centralized management**: Manage all workspaces from a single admin dashboard with cross-workspace analytics
- **CRM per workspace**: Each client gets their own CRM with contacts, tags, and segmentation
- **Campaigns per workspace**: Each client can run independent outbound campaigns
- **Analytics per workspace**: Client-level dashboards with performance metrics

### Synthflow White-Label Model

Synthflow offers purpose-built white-label capabilities designed specifically for agency reselling:

- **Branded dashboards**: Remove all Synthflow branding and apply your agency's logo, colors, and styling
- **Custom domains**: Host the platform on your agency's domain (youragency.com instead of synthflow.ai)
- **Client management**: Create and manage sub-accounts for each client from an agency portal
- **Reseller pricing**: Margin-based pricing that lets agencies mark up services for profit
- **Branded widgets**: Customizable widgets with your agency's branding
- **Client onboarding**: Simplified setup flow for new clients
- **White-label emails**: Notifications sent from your agency's domain

### Agency Feature Verdict

For pure white-label reselling where the primary goal is presenting voice AI under your own brand with custom domains, branded dashboards, and full visual customization, Synthflow has a more polished and purpose-built white-label offering. The agency model is core to Synthflow's design.

SmartAlex's workspace model provides deeper per-client functionality (full CRM, campaign management, analytics, billing per workspace) but is oriented more toward businesses managing voice operations across departments, locations, or client accounts. SmartAlex's white-label is functional but not as visually customizable as Synthflow's agency portal.

**If your primary business model is reselling voice AI to clients under your brand**, Synthflow's white-label is more mature and agency-focused. **If each client needs a full platform experience with CRM, campaigns, and analytics**, SmartAlex's workspace isolation provides more capability per client.

## Migration Considerations

### Moving from Synthflow to SmartAlex

1. **Agent configurations**: Recreate agent flows from Synthflow's builder in SmartAlex's agent builder. The 4 agent modes accelerate setup for common use cases.
2. **Contact data**: Export contacts from Synthflow and import via CSV into SmartAlex's built-in CRM with full tagging and segmentation support.
3. **Client accounts**: Map Synthflow sub-accounts to SmartAlex tenants. Each tenant gets full CRM, campaigns, and analytics.
4. **Telephony**: Phone numbers may need porting to SmartAlex's managed telephony infrastructure.
5. **Zapier automations**: Map Zapier workflows to SmartAlex's webhook system or REST API for more robust automation.
6. **White-label branding**: SmartAlex uses embeddable widgets and per-tenant configuration rather than full dashboard rebranding.

SmartAlex provides migration assistance for Business and Enterprise plan customers.

### Moving from SmartAlex to Synthflow

1. **Contact export**: Export all contacts and campaign data via CSV from SmartAlex's CRM.
2. **Agent prompts**: Copy agent system prompts for recreation in Synthflow's builder.
3. **Campaign history**: Archive campaign analytics and results (Synthflow has more limited campaign tools).
4. **CRM replacement**: Consider a separate CRM if you relied on SmartAlex's built-in CRM.
5. **White-label setup**: Configure Synthflow's agency portal with custom branding and domains.

## Summary

SmartAlex and Synthflow both make AI voice agents accessible without coding, but they serve different market segments. SmartAlex is a deep business platform that integrates voice agents with CRM, campaigns, billing, analytics, leads management, and real estate tools, making it the stronger choice for businesses that need a comprehensive operational platform. Synthflow excels at simplicity and white-label capabilities, making it the better choice for agencies that want to resell voice AI under their own brand with minimal complexity and a polished agency portal.

The decision often comes down to depth versus simplicity. If you need voice agents as part of a broader business workflow with CRM, campaigns, analytics, and security, SmartAlex provides all of that in a single platform with no additional tools required. If you need a straightforward voice agent builder that you can white-label for clients with custom branding and domains, Synthflow delivers a focused experience with less overhead.

For agencies, the choice depends on your value proposition. If you are primarily reselling voice agents and want maximum brand control, Synthflow's white-label is more mature. If you want each client to have a full platform experience with CRM, campaigns, and analytics under their account, SmartAlex's multi-tenancy provides more depth per client.

Both platforms continue to evolve, but their core positioning reflects fundamentally different approaches to the AI voice agent market: SmartAlex as a full business operations platform, Synthflow as an accessible no-code builder with agency-first white-label capabilities.
